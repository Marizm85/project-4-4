About Innovize Solutions Consulting LLC

Innovize Solutions Consulting LLC is a New Jersey-based engineering consulting firm specializing in drone-based imaging, 3D modeling, and construction monitoring. Our mission is to deliver precision and innovation through advanced analytics and technology to roofing, real estate, and construction professionals. With services tailored to contractors, insurance adjusters, and developers, we provide accurate data, improve efficiency, and support compliance with local regulations. Our team is committed to safety, results, and excellence in every project.

Your trusted partner for roofing, construction, and real estate services in New Jersey.
