Roofing Services

Roofing projects demand precision, efficiency, and a focus on safety. At Innovize Solutions Consulting, we provide cutting-edge solutions designed to streamline inspections, enhance project planning, and ensure compliance with industry standards — all while keeping your team safe.

  • Comprehensive Aerial Roof Inspections: High-resolution drone imagery and video provide an accurate overview of roof conditions, including damage, wear, and structural integrity.
  • Detailed Roof Measurements: Precise calculations of roof dimensions—length, width, slope, overhangs, and ridges—delivered in easy-to-read PDF reports for seamless project planning.
  • Damage Assessment Reports: Identify visible issues like cracks, leaks, and material failures with high-definition visuals paired with actionable recommendations.
  • 3D Roof Modeling: Detailed models showcasing roof geometry, including pitch, valleys, and ridges, helping your team visualize the project before starting.
  • Safety-First Approach: Minimize risks by eliminating the need for manual roof climbing and hazardous inspections. Our drone technology ensures inspections are performed from a safe distance without compromising accuracy.
  • Post-Inspection Consultations: One-on-one sessions to discuss findings, prioritize repairs, and ensure alignment with project goals.
  • Progress Monitoring: Regular updates with aerial visuals to ensure compliance with project timelines and standards, preventing costly delays or errors.

Drone technology has redefined how roofing professionals approach inspections. Innovize Solutions Consulting delivers precision, safety, and efficiency, helping your business save time, reduce costs, and achieve superior results.
